Privacy Policy

Welcome to the OBE (Outcome-Based Education) Management System. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of all users including faculty, students, and administrators.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Username and login credentials
  • Faculty and student academic data
  • Course and outcome-related performance data
  • System usage logs for security and analytics

2. How We Use Information

The collected data is used strictly for:

  • Managing academic records and OBE calculations
  • Generating CO/PO attainment reports
  • Improving system performance and usability
  • Ensuring secure access and authentication

3. Data Protection

We implement secure authentication and database protection mechanisms to ensure your data is safe. Access to sensitive data is strictly controlled based on user roles (HOD, Faculty, Admin).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Data is only accessible within authorized institutional users.

5. Cookies & Tracking

This system may use basic session cookies to maintain login sessions. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. User Responsibilities

  • Keep login credentials confidential
  • Do not share access with unauthorized users
  • Ensure correct academic data entry

7. Changes to Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Users are encouraged to review it periodically.
